Paris

This post is written by Savan


The city of love, such a beautiful landscape. This city is a must for you and a partner, put this on your bucket list! The iconic Eifell Tower is the site to see standing at a gigantic 324 metres with three levels for travellers, there are restaurants on the bottom two floors for you to indulge into French pastries.

Travelling to Paris is simple, with many flights daily and also the Eurostar train can get you to Paris within no time from London. Within Paris there is also public transport travel passes for tourists. This city simply cannot be missed.

Dubai

This post is written by Savan


Dubai is the largest city in the United Arab Emirates with a dense population. Its country was only formed 1971, so a fairly new place to visit! It is the fastest growing city worldwide. Dubai is known for its masterpiece called the Burj Khalifa, which is the tallest building worldwide, and it has a staggering 164 floors.

Dubai is the city to visit if you love a good shop, the Dubai Mall is the largest shopping center worldwide that has more than 1,200 stores with plenty of designer stores. When it comes to hotel comfort Dubai is unbeatable, in a league of its own there is a 7-star hotel just off the coast! Yes, 7-star which goes by the name Burj Al Arab.
