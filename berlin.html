Berlin

This post is written by Savan


Berlin, the beautiful German Capital is most certainly a site worth seeing! If you are planning a short trip on a weekend in Europe, Berlin is the destination for you. From viewing amazing city sights to museum hopping, to trying some of the most delicious traditional German burgers, there is something to do for almost everyone! Seasonal Christmas visits are superbly spectacular also with the Christmas Markets.

Berlin has a huge population of 3.7 million and it is the one below the most populous city in the European Union, and a large one third of the city exists as gardens, rivers, lakes, canals and forests. The city is also full of politics, media, science and culture. There is also a Zoological Garden, this is the most visited zoo throughout the whole of Europe.

Berlin Tower

This post is written by Savan


Behold the almighty Berlin Tower, called the Berliner Fernsehturm, is it a television tower built in the Central City. It was constructed between the years of 1965 and 1969. It is a staggering 1207 ft and it is one of the most famous symbols of Germany, many film sets have used this before and also it is one of the top ten popular attractions within Germany. 1,000,000 people visit the tower every year! When the sunlight beams on the tower, there is a stainless teel dome, and the reflection is symbolised as a cross. It is nickednamed as the "Pope'e Revenge".
